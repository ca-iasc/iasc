Empowering under previliged womens

This show provided an opportunity for all the girls to experience stage music and red carpet catwalk on stage held at Ramleela park, Swaroop Nagar, Kanpur(208002), India. It was their first time and all the girls performed very well.

Some girls have great potential to work as models. We urge local businesses to promote these girls in their advertising campaigns for their brands.

Many mothers participated in this show to encourage their young girls. Mr. Govind Singh, a well-known lawyer, and his two sons Yash Khare and Shubham Khare lent a helping hand to arrange the park, sound system, and dinner for all the participants and the audience.

Education is the key to success. Exposure to new careers is essential for better opportunities for the girls. The fashion industry has many fields where girls can find opportunities like sewing, designing, painting, marketing, and modeling.

Our NGO (IASC California) would like to partner with someone who is interested in sack bags (Jhola) and T-shirt manufacturing in this area where local Kanpur home workers can work at their own time while they are going to schools.

All the beautiful clothes and saris for all 40 participants were donated by IASC members from California, Preeta Rolston, Madhu Khare, and Kamini Khare. It is a small step in lending our support to bring some changes in society. Join hands to make a difference one life at a time.

It is the first time this kind of show has been introduced in this community.
